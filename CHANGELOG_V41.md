# Changelog V41

Simplicity and one-action cleanup.

## Changes

- Removed repeated educational/safety callout boxes from the six core steps.
- Standardized the six core step headings to the same format: `Step X of 6: Name`.
- Simplified Step 1 so the setup wizard has one visible action at a time.
- Renamed the wizard's internal button to `Next question` and final button to `Finish setup`.
- Changed Step 1 completion to go directly to Step 2: Checklist instead of routing users to optional tools.
- Kept the six-step product path intact without adding new pages or features.

## Public notes

The site remains an educational organizer only. It does not create legal documents or provide legal advice. Public-test mode remains simple and placeholder monetization/form actions remain hidden or inactive.

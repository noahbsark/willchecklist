Privacy Policy

How this static website may handle contact forms, email signups, analytics, ads, and affiliate technology after setup.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Will Checklist may collect, use, and protect information when you visit the GitHub Pages default site or future custom domain. It is a practical template for a static website and should be reviewed before launch.

Information we may collect

  • Information you submit through forms, such as your name, email address, and message.
  • Newsletter signup information if you connect an email provider.
  • Basic analytics data if you add an analytics provider using ANALYTICS_ID_PLACEHOLDER.
  • Advertising and affiliate interaction data if you add approved ad or affiliate programs.

How information may be used

Information may be used to respond to messages, send requested resources, improve site content, measure performance, prevent abuse, and comply with applicable obligations.

Cookies and analytics

The launch files do not include active analytics or advertising scripts. If you add analytics, advertising, or affiliate technology, those providers may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Email

If you connect NEWSLETTER_ENDPOINT_PLACEHOLDER to an email provider, only send emails to people who requested them and include required unsubscribe options.

Affiliate links and advertising

Some pages may include affiliate placeholders or display ad placeholders. If you activate them, the relevant networks may process click, impression, or conversion data.

Data security

No website can guarantee perfect security. Do not submit passwords, Social Security numbers, bank details, private legal documents, or other sensitive information through this site.

Children

This site is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

Contact

Use the contact page for privacy-related questions.

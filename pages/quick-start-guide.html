The simplest order

Use this site in 5 steps.

  1. Start the setup. It asks one question at a time.
  2. Open the dashboard. Use it as your home base.
  3. Complete the main checklist. Check only what applies.
  4. Use the document locator. Record storage locations, not access details.
  5. Build a planning summary. Print or copy a high-level brief.

Safety rule

Labels and locations only.

Safe: “Life insurance policy - paper copy in blue folder.”

Unsafe: account numbers, policy numbers, passwords, Social Security numbers, safe codes, private keys, seed phrases, or confidential legal strategy.

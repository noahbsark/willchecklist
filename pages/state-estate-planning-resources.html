FAQ

Why does this page link to official directories instead of summarizing state law?

Will execution rules can change and can be fact-specific. This page is designed to help you find official or professional starting points, not provide state-specific legal instructions.

Can I use this page to find free legal help?

It links to USA.gov, LSC, LawHelp, and ABA resources that can help people find legal aid or lawyer-finder resources.

Should I trust search results?

Prefer official court, state government, state bar, legal aid, or licensed professional sources. Avoid copying legal forms from unknown websites.

# Changelog V33 — Calm Resume and Core-Focus Polish

V33 continues the simplicity pass. No new major tools were added.

## What changed

1. The six core pages are calmer:
   - The duplicate mini instruction block is hidden.
   - Step headers are slightly smaller.
   - Safety text is shorter and quieter.
   - The task and one next button stay visually dominant.

2. Continue and Progress pages are simpler:
   - The repeated quick-start strip is hidden on these pages.
   - Decorative hero images are hidden.
   - The pages focus on one next action first.

3. Homepage is quieter:
   - The hero artwork is smaller on desktop and hidden on small screens.
   - Cards are lighter and less visually heavy.

## Simplicity rule

Do not add more tools to the main path. The public flow should remain:

1. Setup
2. Checklist
3. Documents
4. Beneficiaries
5. Summary
6. Final Review

Everything else should stay optional.

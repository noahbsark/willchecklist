# Changelog V58

Focus: make the six-step flow feel easier to review, safer to leave incomplete, and clearer at the moment of moving forward.

## Changes

- Added a short reminder on core pages: users can leave blanks and come back later.
- Changed bottom panels from “done” language to “Ready to move on?” so users do not feel forced to perfectly complete every field.
- Clarified that clicking the next button saves that the step was reviewed in this browser.
- Simplified Step 1 result language.
- Simplified the finish page heading and completion copy.
- Added V58 visual polish for calmer review panels, button contrast, and mobile spacing.

## Public launch note

The public ZIP contains only `/site`. Inactive forms and monetization remain hidden or non-primary for public testing.

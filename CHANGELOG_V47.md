# Changelog V47

Focus: better questions, clearer answers, and a simpler six-step flow.

## Changes

- Reduced Step 2 from a long checklist to shorter, clearer groups.
- Added a direct question prompt to each core step so users know what they are answering.
- Simplified Step 3 document locator wording and examples.
- Made Step 4 beneficiary review labels clearer and safer.
- Simplified Step 5 planning brief to show only core sections by default, with extra saved areas tucked behind an optional drawer.
- Reduced Step 6 final review from 24 items to 12 clearer checks.
- Updated helper text, placeholders, and summary output so users are less likely to enter private numbers or passwords.
